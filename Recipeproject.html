Gulab Jamun



Ingredients:


  • Sugar 300gram
  • Wheat 500gram
  • Milk 100ml
  • Oil 10ml

Steps to Follow:


  1. Boil the oil.
  2. Make sugar syrup by boiling sugar into 500ml water.
  3. Make dough for the gulabjamun by mixing wheat and milk and make round balls.
  4. Fry the balls in hot oil and pour them in sugar syrup.

Berfi



Ingredients:


  • Sugar 300gram
  • Mawa 500gram
  • Milk 100ml
  • ghee 10ml

Steps to Follow:


  1. Boil the milk.
  2. Add Mawa into the milk and stir it continously..
  3. Add sugar into the milk and stir it till it gets thicker.
  4. Now, turn the flame off and let it cool and then cut it into pieces and serve it.

Jalebi



Ingredients:


  • Sugar 300gram
  • Besan 500gram
  • Water 100ml
  • ghee 10ml

Steps to Follow:


  1. Boil the water and add some sugar into it to make sugar syrup.
  2. Make a better of besan and pour it into hot oil using container.
  3. Fry the batter in the oil in round direction.
  4. Then pour those jalebis into sugar syrup and then serve them.
